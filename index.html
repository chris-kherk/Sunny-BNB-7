Monitoring Noise

Sunny BNB
  • At our larger properties or those where the owner feels that it's necessary, we install noise monitoring alarms. These alarms are designed to monitor the noise levels at the property and ensure that guests are not exceeding the set noise limit. The alarms do not record conversations or what people are saying, only the volume of the noise they are making.
  • Our noise monitoring alarms are set to 75Db, which is the maximum volume level we allow at each of our properties. If the property exceeds this limit for more than 10 minutes, the noise monitoring alarm will message our phone to notify us of the noise problem. This allows us to follow up with the guests in question and ensure that they are aware of the noise policy and for them to be aware of the noise problem and act immediately to fix this. After phoning a guest regarding noise levels, we can see immediately whether they have acted on the noise complaint or not, as all data shown is live.

  • One of the amazing features of these hi-tech alarms is that they also count the number of people at the property. The alarms do this by counting the Bluetooth signals from the mobile phones that are present at the property. If the number of guests at the property exceeds what we have set for that particular property, the alarm will once again message our phone to notify us.
  • These noise monitoring alarms are a fantastic safety device for protecting the property owner's asset and minimizing the chances of a guest having a party, event, or function. They are a proactive measure that can help prevent damage to the property and ensure that guests are following the rules.
  • The installation of these alarms is a straightforward process that can be completed quickly and without disruption to guests. We prioritize the installation of these alarms at properties where we feel they are necessary, and the property owner has given us permission to install them. The alarms themselves are small and look like a small smoke alarm. The alarms have a tamper proof function where if anyone removes them from their ceiling mount, or even turns them off, they will message us to say they are being interfered with. Yes we LOVE these alarms.
  • In addition to installing these alarms, we also include a clear statement in each property profile and house rules that we do not accept bookings for events, functions, or parties. This ensures that guests are aware of our policy before they make a booking and helps prevent misunderstandings or disputes.
